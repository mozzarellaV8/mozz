mozzarella.website is an independent publisher specializing in open, ambiguously dynamic editions of in-print, out-of-print, edited, and original work.

On site are color inkjet and monochrome laser printers; a stapler, electric kettle, and wireless internet.

Areas of interest include generative conversations, friendly transactions, scrap + salvage, and questions of commerce and ethics.

Our ultimate goal is to foster continuing exchange of ideas in graceful, yet unceremonious ways.

To acquire, inquire, or count quires: editor@mozzarella.website
