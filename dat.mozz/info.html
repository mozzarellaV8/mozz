mozzarella.website is a press specializing in open, ambiguously dynamic editions of in-print, out-of-print, edited, and original work.

On site are color inkjet and monochrome laser printers; a stapler, electric kettle, and wireless internet.

Areas of interest include generative conversations, friendly transactions, and questions of commerce and ethics. Our ultimate goal is to foster continuing exchange of ideas in graceful, yet unceremonious ways.
